# Code of conduct

PO Editor is committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, constructive, and patient.
- Discuss ideas and code without attacking people.
- Respect different backgrounds, experiences, and viewpoints.
- Give and receive technical feedback in good faith.
- Protect private information shared during support or security discussions.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and publication of
someone else's private information are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `support@nawinds.dev`. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed
to protect the community.
